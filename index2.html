Windfarm locator map

I was assigned what appeared as a simple locator map. However, I worked closely with the reporter in gathering all the acreage map data for land leased to a company creating a windfarm across two Missouri counties. The acreage maps weren’t online, so I had to use physical images of the acreage maps taken by the reporter from Howard County’s acreage map book.

I traced over each individual piece of land and ensured that each trace was proportional, as the images were taken at different distances and angles. I’m especially proud of this graphic, as it hides all that complexity with a simple and clear design.
